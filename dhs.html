Sentiment Analysis Parser - IEEE and NASA Standards



Training models used



  1. Categorical - IEEE Standards Abstracts Model: a model trained by extracting abstracts from IEEE standards and using the most common keyword in the respective standard as its category (14 categories).
  2. Categorical - IEEE Standards Abstracts Model with merged categories: a model trained by extracting abstracts from IEEE standards and using the most common keyword in the respective standard as its category (12 categories).
  3. Categorical - NASA Technical Standards Model: a model trained by taking 80% of the given 42 NASA technical standards (=> 33), extracting the Scope of each and using the division number in the respective standard as its category (9 categories).




Datasets evaluated



  1. IEEE Standards Abstracts (new): the new (different) abstracts from IEEE standards.
  2. NASA Technical Standards (20% of 42): the remaining 9 standards' scopes.
